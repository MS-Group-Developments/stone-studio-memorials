Who we are

Welsh craft.
Natural stone.
No shortcuts.

Stone Studio │ Memorials is the memorial division of Stone Studio — the six-generation Welsh stonemasonry studio behind work for King Charles III, National Trust properties and Oxford colleges.

We work directly with private families and through the funeral trade: funeral directors, crematoria, natural burial grounds and pet cremation services. Both routes receive the same materials, the same workshop, and the same standard of lettering and finish.

Every memorial is carved or engraved by hand in Cardiff. Stone is sourced directly from British quarries wherever the species and the client's wishes allow.

Hand-carved or laser-engraved lettering. Traditional and contemporary forms.

Granite, Portland limestone, Welsh slate, sandstone and marble. Upright headstones, flat markers, kerb sets and bespoke forms. Design consultation, material samples, full installation service.

  • Flat grave markers and ledger stones
  • Natural burial markers (regulation-compliant)
  • Kerb sets and full memorial suites

Natural Burial Ground Memorials

Regulation-compliant. Naturally weathering. In keeping with the land.

We have supplied memorials to Leedam, Sun Rising and other natural burial grounds across the UK. We understand their regulations and design within them. Un-dressed natural stone, native stone species, minimal intervention.

  • Undressed natural field stone
  • Simple engraved Welsh slate markers
  • Regulation compliance by ground
  • No chemical treatments or sealants
  • Naturally biodiverse stone forms
